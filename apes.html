The adaptable poly engineering simulator framework (APES)


The APES framework is developed and maintained at the Chair for Simulation Techniques and Scientific Computing of the University of Siegen, Germany. I have contributed to parts of the solver, especially the LBM solver Musubi. Components of the framework have been tested for scalability on all the major supercomputers (Tier-0) in Europe including the SuperMUC-NG, Hazel Hen, Juqueen, Piz Daint.
Most of the Co∅ projects use and develop one or more components of this framework and it forms the backbone of my research. Various members of APES are available as open sources packages that can be downloaded here.
